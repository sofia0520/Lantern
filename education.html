UiTM Machang

This is where i study, which is Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM). UiTm Machang. Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) began in 1956 as Dewan Latehan RIDA (RIDA Training Centre) in Petaling Jaya. Their goal is uplifting rural Malays with about 50 students, which then evolve into Malaysia's largest public university system by expanding nationwide with numerous campuses, offering hundreds of programs, and growing from its initial focus on technical skills to a comprehensive institution for diverse fields, significantly impacting Malaysian higher education. Meanwhile, UiTM Cawangan Kelantan (UiTM Kelantan Branch) was established in July 1985 as a temporary location near Kota Bharu. However, it officially moved to its permanent campus in Bukit Ilmu, Machang, in January 1996. There are many courses offered by Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) such as:
